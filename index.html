Official Notice of Dissolution

Revatek has been officially and legally dissolved and has permanently ceased all business operations. Thank you to everyone who supported us during our years of operation.

Support & Warranties

Operations Concluded:
Following the formal dissolution of the corporate entity, all customer support, technical troubleshooting, hardware replacements, and warranty services have terminated. There is no active entity or support team available to service past products or manage accounts.

Product Status:

  • Technical Support: Unmonitored. No responses will be provided to incoming support emails or messages.
  • Revatek Connect / Online Services: All active cloud infrastructure and connected monitoring services have been permanently discontinued.
  • Documentation: The archival technology has been left open-source for the community (link below).

About Revatek

Revatek was founded in 2024 to provide innovative battery charging solutions for off-grid lifestyles. Our flagship product, the Altion alternator regulator, helped marine and RV enthusiasts optimize their battery charging systems.

Open Source Repository: The core technology has been open-sourced in hopes that the community will continue to leverage and adapt it. You can find the public repository here: GitHub - Revatek Alternator Regulator.

Thank you for being part of our journey.
