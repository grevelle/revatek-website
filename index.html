About Revatek

Revatek was founded in 2024 to provide innovative battery charging solutions for off-grid lifestyles. Our flagship product, the Altion alternator regulator, helped marine and RV enthusiasts optimize their battery charging systems.

Open Source: We plan to open source the technology so others can continue the mission of providing innovative battery charging solutions for off-grid lifestyles.
